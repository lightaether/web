Battleship Rules

1. The game is played on a grid of 10x10 squares.

2. Each player has five ships: an aircraft carrier, a battleship, a cruiser, a submarine, and a destroyer.

3. The objective is to locate and destroy all of the opponent's ships.

4. Players take turns guessing the location of the opponent's ships.

5. When a player guesses a location, the opponent reveals whether it was a hit or a miss.
